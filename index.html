Our bikes

Kawasaki Ninja H2R (New & Used)

The Ninja H2R is a supercharged monster, delivering an insane 310+ HP from its 998cc inline-four engine. Built exclusively for the track, it has carbon-fibre winglets for extreme aerodynamics and is one of the fastest production motorcycles in the world.

Ducati Panigale V4 R (New & Used)

A track-focused beast, the Panigale V4 R comes with a 998cc Desmosedici Stradale R engine producing 240.5 HP (with race exhaust). It features advanced aerodynamics, lightweight construction, and MotoGP-inspired technology, making it one of the most powerful production superbikes.

BMW M 1000 RR (New & Used)

BMW’s M-series superbike, the M 1000 RR, is a 999cc powerhouse producing 212 HP. Designed for track performance, it boasts carbon-fibre winglets, lightweight construction, and cutting-edge electronics, ensuring exceptional speed and agility.

Yamaha YZF-R1M (New & Used)

Inspired by MotoGP technology, the Yamaha YZF-R1M features a 998cc crossplane engine generating 200 HP. It comes with Ohlins electronic suspension, a carbon-fibre body, and advanced rider aids, making it a superbike built for precision and performance.

Suzuki Hayabusa (New & Used)

The legendary Hayabusa is known for its speed and stability. Powered by a 1340cc inline-four engine with 187 HP, it delivers exceptional top-speed capabilities while offering superior aerodynamics, smooth handling, and long-distance comfort.
